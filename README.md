# easyHATS

### HATS pack with minimal software - No more unnecessary homebrew - No more atrocious customization
I have to mantain several consoles and their users want something easy, fast, clean and pretty. I found this is the easiest way to keep them updated. They don´t care about fancy homebrew or amazing features. They just want it to WORK. And me too :)

Included programs:
- Atmosphere.
- Hekate.
- AIO Switch Updater.
- Quick Reboot app.
- Sphaira.
- Switch 90DNS Tester.
- MissionControl.
- Sigpatches.
- HB App Store.
- Tinfoil.
- nx-ovlloader.
- Tesla-Menu.
- QuickNTP.

Additional features:
- DNS.MITM.
- Exosphere.
- Custom hekate_ipl.ini (just changing some names, no big deal).

# HOWTO
1. Turn off your console, extract the SD card and insert into your card reader.
2. Grab and unzip the latest release.
3. Copy all the contents from the "SWJRR Pack" folder into the root of your SD Card.
4. Safely eject your SD card.
5. Insert into your console and turn it on as usually.
